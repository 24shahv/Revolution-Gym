Who We Are

THE FIRE
WITHIN

Revolution Gym wasn't built to be just another fitness centre. It was built to be a movement — a declaration that Nagpur deserves world-class fitness infrastructure, elite coaching talent, and a training culture that rivals any city in India.


Founded in 2024 by a team of competitive athletes and sports scientists, we invested in 15,000 sq.ft of premium training space in the heart of Dharampeth — equipped with machinery that most cities don't see outside of professional sports facilities.


From Day 1, our philosophy has been simple: treat every member like an athlete, regardless of where they're starting from. Whether you've never lifted a weight or you're training for a national competition — your revolution starts here.

Our Journey

THE
TIMELINE

✦
2022
The Idea

Founders Arjun Deshmukh and Priya Kulkarni — both competitive athletes — return to Nagpur and find the city's fitness infrastructure severely lacking. The vision for Revolution Gym is born.

✦
2023
Laying the Foundation

15,000 sq.ft space acquired in Dharampeth. Equipment imported from Germany and the US. 6 elite coaches recruited from Mumbai, Pune, and Delhi. The build begins.

✦
2024
Doors Open

Revolution Gym opens its doors. 200 founding members on Day 1. The city responds. Within 90 days, we cross 1,000 active members and launch our first HIIT programme.

✦
2024
MMA Studio Launch

Dedicated 2,000 sq.ft MMA & Combat studio added. Partnership with former state-level champions. Nagpur's first structured amateur boxing programme launched.

★
Now
5,000+ Strong

Over 5,000 active members. 20+ certified coaches. 24/7 access. Nagpur's #1 rated gym. The revolution is just getting started.
