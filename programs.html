Strength & Power

Beginner → Elite

Built on the big compound lifts — squat, bench, deadlift, overhead press. Our periodised programming ensures every member progresses safely and sustainably toward their strength potential.

  • Olympic lifting platforms
  • Conjugate & linear periodisation
  • Powerlifting & bodybuilding tracks
  • Progress tracking every 4 weeks

🔥

HIIT & Burn

All Levels

45-minute high-intensity sessions designed to maximise caloric burn, spike EPOC, and build cardiovascular capacity. No two sessions are the same. Pure intensity, maximum results.

  • 7 group sessions per week
  • Heart rate zone training
  • Metabolic conditioning circuits
  • Tabata, AMRAP & EMOM formats
03
🥊

MMA & Combat

Intermediate+

Boxing, Muay Thai, and kickboxing conditioning taught by coaches with pro fight records. Builds full-body explosive power, coordination, and mental toughness in a structured environment.

  • Pro boxing ring on-site
  • Pad work in small groups (max 6)
  • Sparring available for advanced
  • Amateur competition pathways
04
🧘

Yoga & Recovery

All Levels

200-hr RYT certified instructors blending traditional yoga with modern sports recovery science. Designed specifically for athletes and high-intensity trainers who need smart active recovery.

  • 5 yoga classes per week
  • Athletes' mobility programme
  • Pranayama breathwork
  • Dedicated air-conditioned studio
05
🚴

Cardio & Conditioning

All Levels

A 3,000 sq.ft cardio arena with 20+ treadmills, 12 cycles, 8 rowing machines, 6 assault bikes, stair climbers, and ski ergs. Structured programmes or open use — your choice.

  • Open 24 hours
  • Structured endurance programmes
  • Race & event prep available
  • VO2 max testing on request

🏆

Personal Training

Premium · All Levels

1-on-1 sessions with Nagpur's most qualified coaches. Custom periodised programming, nutrition planning, and accountability. The fastest path to any fitness goal, period.

  • Bespoke programme design
  • Nutrition & meal plan integration
  • Monthly body composition checks
  • Priority booking & private sessions
